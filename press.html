Acasta Gneiss
"Glimmer"

Iridescence is an optical property where the color of an object is determined by the angle of the viewer, often observed in a puddle of an oil spill, soap bubbles or the surface of a compact disc. The seven sparse pieces on Acasta Gneissâ€™ Glimmer have a similar quality. Building on repetitive structures these compositions drift in and out of focus, seemingly changing with each iteration. As the album progresses ethereal synth pads are contrasted with recordings inspired by asmr practices, heavily processed using digital techniques. Glimmer offers an immersive, sometimes unnerving, but ultimately hopeful experience, like a faint, wavering light in the distance illuminating the darkness.

Glimmer is available on November 26 2023, digitally and on a limited run of 30C cassette tapes. The album will also be available with a limited edition booklet containing additional text and artwork.

About the artist:
Acasta Gneiss is an experimental music producer, programmer and DJ based in Brussels. His minimal compositions are built from recurring structures that aim to explore how we construct our reality. His sophomore album entitled Glimmer will be out on Anterior Insula on November 26th 2023. He is the co-founder of Anterior Insula and resident DJ at Kiosk Radio.
